Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Private PDF Deck is designed to process documents locally on your Mac.

Data Collection

Private PDF Deck does not collect personal data.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, or user accounts.

Document Processing

PDFs and images opened with Private PDF Deck are processed on your device. The app does not upload documents to a server.

The app reads and writes only files or folders selected by the user through standard macOS open, save, and folder selection panels.

OCR

Private PDF Deck includes optional OCR features for image pages. OCR is performed locally using Apple system frameworks.

Network Access

The main document editing features of Private PDF Deck do not require network access.

Settings

Private PDF Deck stores app settings, such as export defaults and viewer preferences, in the local macOS app settings area.

Support

For support, bug reports, and feature requests, please contact contact.tabitabijourney@gmail.com.
